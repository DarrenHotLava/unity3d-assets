PACKAGE TITLE
==============================
Profile - Facebook, Twitter, Google+ Social SDK


PACKAGE DESCRIPTION
==============================
Socialize your games! Our Unity <strong>Profile</strong> plugin is the best tool to integrate Social networks into your game.
We support <strong>Facebook</strong>,  <strong>Twitter</strong> and <strong>Google+</strong> ! All major social networks in one plugin with the same API!

<br>
<br>
** Thousands of games already use SOOMLA **
<br>
<br>
<a href="http://soom.la">Website</a> | <a href="http://know.soom.la/unity/profile/">Docs</a> | <a href="http://know.soom.la/docs/university/videos/">Videos</a> | <a href="http://answers.soom.la">Forum</a>
<br>
<br>
With this plugin you can boost your game with:<br>
- Social Login<br>
- Like<br>
- Rate App<br>
- Invite Friends<br>
- Share Status<br>
- Upload Image<br>
- Get Contacts<br>
- Get Feed<br>
<br>
<strong>All with one unified API for all social networks.</strong>
<br>
<br>


In addition to easy integration with multiple social providers this library will provide you with encrypted on-device storage that will keep the user's data after closing the game. SOOMLA for Unity is available for <strong>iOS and Android</strong>.

<br>
