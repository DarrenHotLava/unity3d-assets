PACKAGE TITLE
==============================
SOOMLA Unity Store - In App Purchase


PACKAGE DESCRIPTION
==============================
All free to play games use IAP to monetize. SOOMLA offers you a free and easy way to do this. Our Unity <strong>in-app purchaes</strong> plugin is the best tool to model your virtual economy and integrate IAP into your game.
<br>
<br>
** Thousands of games already use SOOMLA **
<br>
<br>
<a href="http://soom.la">Website</a> | <a href="http://know.soom.la/unity/store/">Docs</a> | <a href="http://know.soom.la/docs/university/videos/">Videos</a> | <a href="http://answers.soom.la">Forum</a><br>
<br>
In addition to modeling and easy to use IAP this plugin will provide you with encrypted on-device storage and a single API to do all your virtual economy operations. SOOMLA for Unity is available for <strong>iOS / Android / Amazon</strong>.
