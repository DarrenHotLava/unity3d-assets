PACKAGE TITLE
==============================
In App Purchase Builder


PACKAGE DESCRIPTION
==============================
Easily add in app purchase to your game with our editor extension.  Sell coins, "remove ads", or any other virtual good. This plugin comes with the <a href="https://www.assetstore.unity3d.com/en/#!/content/6103">SOOMLA Store package</a> baked in, and compliments it with an editor extension for building virtual economies without coding.  Here's what you get:<br><br>
- An editor extension to build your economy<br>
- A rich economy model<br>
- Simple management of user balances<br>
- Encrypted on-device storage<br>
- Supported billing: Apple, Google & Amazon<br>
- One API for all platforms<br>
<br>
<br>
If you already use SOOMLA, this plugin is exactly what you need.  All free to play games use IAP to monetize. SOOMLA offers you a free and easy way to do this. Our Unity <strong>in-app purchase</strong> plugin is the best tool to model your virtual economy and integrate IAP into your game.
